President Underwood launches new America Works rollout

Frank Underwood has announced the second phase of his flagship America Works program.

The scheme, which was a key campaign point in Underwood’s 2016 campaign, will begin a new stage of rollout later this Spring, starting in Southern states.

The President used the announcement to declare unemployment across the country a “growing threat to our economy and our country’s future”.

His decision to relaunch the controversial program has been met with strong criticism from the Republican party.
